GOTO Statement

The PL/SQL GOTO statement is a squential control structure available in oracle. The GOTO statement immediately program control (called 'branching') unconditionally to a named statement label or block label. The statement or label name must be unique in the block.

  • The Syntax of GOTO Statement

    • GOTO label;
    label_name


  • The Example of GOTO Statement

    • begin
    Dbms_output.put_line('Fist line');
    goto abc;
    Dbms_output.put_line('Second line');
    abc
    Dbms_output.put_line('Third line');
    Dbms_output.put_line('Four line');
    end;
